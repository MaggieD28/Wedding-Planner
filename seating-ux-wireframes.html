Canvas now shows who is sitting where — at a glance across the whole room

New Each table shows guest first names on seat dots radiating around it — pink for Bride side, green for Groom side, warm red for any conflicts. Empty seats show as grey dashed dots. The Head Table shows seats above and below the rectangle. Drag any table to reposition it in the room; the names move with it.
